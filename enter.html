Rules for Chess:-

1. Each and every player would be provided with 5 mins each.
2. The clock would be placed at the left hand side of the white king.
3. When the match is going on, a person can use only 1 hand to move his pieces and click on the clock. If a player is found using 2 hands by the volunteers, the player would lose 2 seconds on his clock.
4. 3 fouls leads to loosing the match by the player.
5. The opponent should call the volunteers as soon as a player plays a foul move.
6. Each win would be awarded 2 points each.
7. In case the timer stops for one player, the opponent wins the match.
8. In case of a stalemate, each player would be awarded 1 point each.
9. Any case of unnecessary arguments with the players and the volunteers would result in a loss of 2 points.
10. The match wont be a knockout match.
11. The player with the highest number of points would be qualified for finals.
12. Top 2 players would receive a cash prize.
13. Last minute entries would not be entertained.
